Welcome to Kosovo

Discover the hidden beauty of the Balkans – from breathtaking mountains and crystal lakes to vibrant culture and history.

Highlights of Kosovo

Prizren

The cultural capital with cobbled streets, Ottoman mosques, and the famous castle overlooking the city.

Rugova Canyon

A natural wonder near Peja, perfect for hiking, climbing, and enjoying breathtaking views.

Sharr Mountains

Home to glacial lakes, snowy peaks, and rich biodiversity — a paradise for nature lovers.

Why Visit Kosovo?

Kosovo is a land of untouched nature, rich history, and welcoming people. Whether you love hiking, history, or exploring culture, Kosovo has something unique to offer.
